Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026

ErrorLookup is a mostly static developer reference website focused on HTTP status codes, troubleshooting guidance, and code comparisons. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use the site and how that information is handled.

Information We May Collect

When you browse ErrorLookup, we may receive technical request data such as IP address, user agent, referrer URL, requested path, timestamp, and basic response metadata. This information is commonly generated by web servers, CDN infrastructure, and hosting providers to operate and secure the site.

If you contact us by email, we receive the information you include in your message, such as your email address, subject line, and request details.

Analytics Usage

We may use analytics tools to understand site performance and content usefulness, for example page views, approximate geography, traffic sources, and high-level engagement patterns. This helps us improve popular resources such as status code lookup pages, client error references, and troubleshooting guides.

Analytics reporting is used in aggregate to improve developer documentation quality rather than to profile individual users.

Logs, CDN, and Server Data

ErrorLookup may use CDN and hosting infrastructure that temporarily stores access logs for performance, reliability, abuse prevention, and security monitoring. These logs can include network and request-level information needed to detect malicious traffic and troubleshoot availability issues.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

ErrorLookup may use cookies or similar browser storage through analytics and advertising integrations to support measurement and site operation. Depending on your location and browser settings, you may be able to limit certain cookies through browser controls or privacy tools.

Search and On-Site Usage Signals

When you use site search or navigation pathways, we may collect aggregate usage signals (such as commonly searched status codes) to improve content organization and discoverability. We use this data to reduce friction in common debugging journeys.

Third-Party Services

We may rely on third-party infrastructure and tools for analytics, hosting, security, and advertising support. These providers may process data according to their own policies when requests pass through their systems.

ErrorLookup may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

Data Retention

We retain operational logs and related technical records only as long as reasonably necessary for security, reliability, and business operations. Retention periods may vary by system and provider.

Email correspondence is retained as needed to respond to requests, maintain support continuity, and document policy-related communications.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information processed through ErrorLookup. No internet transmission or storage method is completely secure, but we work to minimize risk and limit unnecessary collection.

Children's Privacy

ErrorLookup is intended for a developer audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when site practices, legal requirements, or infrastructure providers change. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date when applicable.
