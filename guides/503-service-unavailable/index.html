HTTP 503 Service Unavailable

Plain-English meaning

Service temporarily unavailable.

Why it happens

Maintenance windows, autoscaling cold start, queue saturation, overloaded workers..

Common causes

  • Misconfiguration in gateway or app layer
  • Stale deploy or environment drift
  • Unexpected traffic pattern during incident

How to fix it

Scale capacity, drain traffic safely, and return Retry-After during maintenance..

Example request/response or environment

HTTP/1.1 503 Service Unavailable
Retry-After: 120

Browser/dev/server context

Preferred for planned downtime instead of generic 500.
