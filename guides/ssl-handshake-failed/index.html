SSL Handshake Failed

Plain-English meaning

TLS negotiation failed before any HTTP exchange.

Why it happens

Most often due to DNS, TLS policy, certificate mismatch, or handshake protocol incompatibility depending on client and edge path.

What to check first

Capture browser error details, validate DNS and certificate chain, then verify load balancer and origin TLS configuration.

How to fix it

Correct DNS/certificate settings, align TLS versions and ciphers, and ensure SNI routes to the expected certificate and service.
